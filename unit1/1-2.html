1.3 Why do people go on holiday outside HK?

People travel outside Hong Kong for recreation:

  • Visiting attractions
  • Experiencing culture
  • Joining sports and music events
  • Enjoying food and shopping
  • Relaxing
  • Visiting relatives and friends

1.4 What makes it possible for HK people to travel outside HK?

A) High income level

HK people generally have a high income, so they can afford to travel.

B) Long holidays

  • By law, HK people enjoy paid annual leave and public holidays
  • The five-day work week allows people to plan long holidays

C) Convenient and cheaper transport

  • Well-developed transport network
  • Budget airlines are popular and offer cheaper airfares than traditional carriers
  • Kai Tak Cruise Terminal develops cruise tourism
  • GZ-SZ-HK high-speed rail and HK-Zh-Macao Bridge provide direct links to the mainland
  • These increase short and medium distance trips to the mainland

D) Advertisements and TV programmes

They enrich people's knowledge of other places and inspire travel.

E) One-stop travel services

  • Travel agencies and websites provide one-stop services
  • People can get travel information, book transport, hotel, and local tours easily
  • This saves time and effort
👨‍🏫 Teacher's note: For "enabling factors" questions, memorise all five factors (A–E). The most detailed ones to explain are high income level, convenient transport (especially budget airlines and high-speed rail), and one-stop travel services.
