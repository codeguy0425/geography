1.1 What is recreation? Why is it important?

Definition

The activities we do during our leisure time are called recreational activities.

Importance

  • Working and studying make people tired and stressed
  • We need to refresh ourselves and balance our life by having recreational activities
  • This helps keep our minds and bodies healthy

1.2 Characteristics of HK people going on holiday outside HK

A) Number of people

More and more people are travelling outside Hong Kong.

B) Time

  • People usually travel to other places during long holidays (e.g. Easter, Christmas)
  • These are called peak seasons
  • The airport becomes very crowded during these periods
