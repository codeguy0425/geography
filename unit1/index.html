Key terms — quick revision cards

Recreation
Activities done during leisure time to refresh and balance life
Leisure time
Free time when not working or studying
Peak season
Periods when many people travel, such as Easter and Christmas
Budget airline
Low-cost airlines offering cheaper airfares than traditional carriers
High-speed rail
Fast railway connecting cities, e.g. Guangzhou–Shenzhen–Hong Kong Express Rail Link
HK-Zh-Macao Bridge
Bridge connecting Hong Kong, Zhuhai, and Macao, reducing travel time
Kai Tak Cruise Terminal
Cruise terminal in Hong Kong that develops cruise tourism
One-stop travel service
Service that allows booking transport, hotel, and tours all in one place
Paid annual leave
Paid time off from work that employees are entitled to by law
Travel agency
A business that arranges travel and accommodation for tourists

C. Enjoying food and shopping
D. Joining sports events

A. It increases working hours
B. It refreshes our minds and keeps us healthy
C. It reduces leisure time
D. It makes people more stressed
Q3
Which of the following makes it possible for HK people to travel abroad?
A. Limited transport options
B. High income level and paid annual leave
C. Fewer public holidays
D. Expensive airfares

True or False (1 mark each)

Q4
"Recreation is only for physical health."
False — recreation keeps both our minds and bodies healthy.
Q5
"Budget airlines make travel more expensive."
False — budget airlines offer cheaper airfares, making travel more affordable.

Structured Question (6 marks)

Q6

(a) Explain why recreation is important. (2 marks)

(b) Describe two factors that enable Hong Kong people to travel outside Hong Kong. (4 marks)

Suggested structure:
(a) Recreation helps refresh ourselves after work or study, which makes us tired and stressed. It balances our life and keeps our minds and bodies healthy.
(b) Factor 1: High income level — HK people generally have a high income, so they can afford to travel. Factor 2: Convenient transport — budget airlines offer cheaper airfares; the high-speed rail and HK-Zh-Macao Bridge provide direct links to the mainland, making short trips easy.
