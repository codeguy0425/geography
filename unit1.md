# Unit 1 — Why Do People Travel to Other Places?

> **Exam weighting:** Appears in Section C (Structured Question).
> Expect a question asking you to explain factors enabling HK people to travel or reasons why recreation is important.

---

## 1.1 What is recreation? Why is it important?

### Definition

Recreation refers to activities done during **leisure time** — time free from work, study, or other duties.

### Importance of recreation

- **Reduces stress** from work and study — helps people relax and unwind
- **Refreshes the mind and body** — improves physical and mental well-being
- **Balances life** — creates a healthy balance between work/study and personal time

---

## 1.2 Characteristics of HK people going on holiday

### A) More and more people travelling outside HK

The number of Hong Kong residents travelling abroad has been increasing steadily. Rising affluence and cheaper transport make overseas trips more accessible.

### B) Travel during long holidays (peak seasons)

Most HK people travel during long holidays such as **Easter** and **Christmas**. These are **peak seasons** when the airport is crowded and flight prices are higher.

---

## 1.3 Why do people go on holiday outside HK?

People travel abroad for various reasons related to **recreation**:

- **Visiting attractions** — sightseeing at famous landmarks and natural wonders
- **Experiencing culture** — learning about local customs, traditions, and ways of life
- **Joining events** — attending festivals, concerts, sports events, and exhibitions
- **Food and shopping** — trying local cuisine and shopping for unique products
- **Relaxing** — escaping daily routines to rest and recharge
- **Visiting relatives and friends** — reuniting with family and friends living overseas

---

## 1.4 What makes it possible for HK people to travel?

| Factor | Explanation |
|--------|------------|
| **A) High income level** | HK has a high GDP per capita — people can afford travel expenses such as flights, accommodation, and spending money |
| **B) Long holidays** | Paid annual leave and the 5-day work week give workers enough time for overseas trips |
| **C) Convenient and cheaper transport** | Budget airlines offer low-cost flights; Kai Tak Cruise Terminal provides cruise options; high-speed rail connects HK to Mainland China; HK-Zh-Macao Bridge shortens travel time to Macao and Zhuhai |
| **D) Advertisements and TV programmes** | Media exposure promotes travel destinations and inspires people to visit new places |
| **E) One-stop travel services** | Travel agencies and websites offer packaged tours, flight bookings, hotel reservations, and travel insurance all in one place, making trip planning easy |

> 👨‍🏫 **Teacher's note:** For "explain" questions (3–4 marks), choose **two** factors and give a specific example for each. For example: "Convenient and cheaper transport — budget airlines like HK Express offer low-cost fares, making air travel affordable for more people."

## Exam-style practice questions

### Multiple Choice (1 mark each)

1. Which of the following is **NOT** considered a recreational activity?
   - A. Hiking in a country park
   - B. **Working overtime**
   - C. Visiting a museum
   - D. Playing sports

2. Why is recreation important for people?
   - A. It increases working hours
   - B. **It refreshes the mind and body**
   - C. It reduces leisure time
   - D. It raises income levels

3. Which factor enables HK people to travel abroad more easily?
   - A. Low income and short holidays
   - B. **High income and paid annual leave**
   - C. Fewer budget airlines
   - D. Lack of travel agencies

### True or False (1 mark each)

4. "Recreation is only important for physical health, not mental health." **F** — recreation benefits both physical and mental health by reducing stress and refreshing the mind.

5. "Budget airlines make overseas travel more expensive." **F** — budget airlines offer lower fares by charging only for basic services and adding fees for extras.

### Structured Question (6 marks)

> (a) Explain why recreation is important for people. (2 marks)
>
> (b) Describe **two** factors that make it possible for Hong Kong people to travel abroad. (4 marks)

**Suggested answer:**

**(a)** Recreation reduces stress from work and study (1 mark) and refreshes the mind and body, helping people maintain a balanced life (1 mark).

**(b)** (1) **High income level** — Hong Kong has a high GDP per capita, so many people can afford flights, accommodation, and other travel expenses. (2) **Convenient and cheaper transport** — budget airlines (e.g. HK Express) offer low-cost flights, and the high-speed rail connects HK to Mainland cities quickly and affordably.
