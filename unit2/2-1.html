2.1 What Benefits Do Tourists Bring to the Host Area?

E7 Tourism · S1 Geography Final Revision

Economic benefits

  • Provide jobs and incomes to local people (hotels, restaurants, tour guides, transport)
  • Increase tax revenue for local governments (spending on public services)
  • Improve infrastructure and attract more investment (airports, roads, utilities)

Social benefits

  • Develop infrastructure that also benefits local residents
  • Provide more social services and financial aid
  • Raise the living standard of local people

Environmental benefits

  • Protect the natural environment and wildlife (national parks, conservation areas)
  • Raise public awareness of environmental conservation

Cultural benefits

  • Promote cultural exchange between tourists and locals
  • Preserve cultural heritage (historic buildings, traditional festivals, handicrafts)
👨‍🏫 Teacher's note: When answering "benefits of tourism" questions, organise your answer by the four categories: economic, social, environmental, and cultural. This structure is easy for examiners to follow.
